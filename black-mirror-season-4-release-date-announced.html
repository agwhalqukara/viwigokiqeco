Black Mirror Season 4 Release Date Announced

Netflix has announced a release date for the fourth season of Black Mirror.

Six brand new episodes of the sci-fi anthology series are scheduled to air just before the end of the year, on the 29th December 2017.

https://twitter.com/NetflixUK/status/938424082768338947

Black Mirror focuses on satirical and psychological themes surrounding modern society and the potential consequences of new technologies. Episodes are stand-alone but tend to be set in an alternate reality or in the near future.

In the 12 days before the release date announcement, teasers for each of the six episodes were revealed. You can check out the teaser clips for “Arkangel“, “Crocodile“, “Black Museum“, “Hang The DJ“, “Metalhead” and “U.S.S. Callister” by clicking here.

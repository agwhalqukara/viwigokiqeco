Sep 8, 2024 Larita Shotwell

True Vine Hotel, Kumasi, Ghana

We arrived to some very welcoming and friendly staff who swiftly showed us to our room and stayed briefly to make sure we were happy with everything.
The AC was struggling a bit, and made too much noise so we had it turned off but the weather wasn’t too hot outside so the room itself was not too hot.
I am disappointed that the level of decor for the rooms did not seem to be carried through to the bathroom.
The bathroom was more reminiscent of a lower class hotel albeit of a big size.
Food was great, but we were sadly frustrated by the music from their weekend Pool Jamz.

Address:

True Vine Hotel

Plot 22 Blk E, Nhyiaeso, Ghana,
Kumasi,

Ghana

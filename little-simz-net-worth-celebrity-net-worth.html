What is Little Simz's Net Worth?

Little Simz is an English rapper, musician, singer, and actress who has a net worth of $2 million. Little Simz was born in Islington, North London, England in 1994. She released the mixtapes STRATOSPHERE in 2010, STRATOSPHERE 2 in 2011, and XY.ZED and BLANK CANVAS in 2013. Simz also released the Extended plays E.D.G.E , AGE 101: DROP 1, AGE 101: DROP 2, AGE 101: Drop 3 in 2014 as well as AGE 101: DROP 4, THE THEORY OF…, and AGE 101: DROP X in 2015.

Her debut studio album A Curious Tale of Trials + Persons in was released in 2015. As an actress Little Simz starred as Vicky in the television series Spirit Warriors in 2010 and as Meeka in the TV series Youngers in 2013. In 2015 she was nominated for a BET Award for Best International Act: UK.

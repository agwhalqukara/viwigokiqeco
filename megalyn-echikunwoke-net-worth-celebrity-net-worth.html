What is Megalyn Echikunwoke's Net Worth?

Megalyn Echikunwoke is an American actress who has a net worth of $3 million. Megalyn Echikunwoke was born in Spokane, Washington in May 1983. In 2001 she starred as Cherish Pardee on the television series Spyder Games. Echikunwoke had a recurring role on the TV series 24 as Nicole Palmer from 2001 to 2002. She starred as Danika Ward on the series Like Family from 2003 to 2004. From 2004 to 2005 she had a recurring role as Angie Barnett on the television series That '70s Show. Echikunwoke starred as Isabelle Tyler on the TV series The 4400 from 2006 to 2007.

From 2008 to 2009 she starred as Tara Price on the series CSI: Miami. Echikunwoke had recurring roles as Holly Strickler on the television series 90210 from 2011 to 2012, April on the TV series House of Lies in 2012, and Riley Parker on the series Made in Jersey in 2012. In 2014 she starred as Megan on the television series Mind Games. Echikunwoke had a recurring role on the TV series The Following as Penny in 2015 and starred as Simone Baptiste on the series Damien in 2016. She voiced the role of Mari McCabe / Vixen on the TV series Vixen from 2015 to 2016 and on the series Arrow since 2016. Megalyn has been romantically linked to actor Chris Rock.

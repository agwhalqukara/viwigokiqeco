Who Is Tom Hanks? Tom Hanks Wiki

Tom Hanks Wiki:- Thomas Jeffrey Hanks, better known as Tom Hanks, is an American actor and film producer. He was born on 9 July 1956 in Concord, California, United States. He is one of the world's most famous and well-known film stars and is considered an American cultural icon for his comedic and dramatic roles. Hanks studied theater at Chabot College in Hayward, California. and after two years transferred to California State University, Sacramento.

 Tom Hanks started his acting career in theatre. In the year 1980, he played the role of Elliot in the film He Knows You're Distant from Everyone. In Daniel Southern's production of Machiavelli's The Mandrake, he was given the role of Callimaco. The following year, Hanks was cast as Kip Wilson in the ABC television pilot Bosom Buddies.

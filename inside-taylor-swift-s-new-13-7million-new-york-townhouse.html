Inside Taylor Swift's new 13.7million New York townhouse

The Look What You Made Me Do singer has added to her property portfolio

    Look what you made her do! Taylor Swift has snapped up a new townhouse in New York for $18million (around £13.7million), and it's just a few doors away from the penthouse she already owns in the trendy neighbourhood of Tribeca, where her friends Blake Lively and Ryan Reynolds live, along with ex-boyfriends Jake Gyllenhaal and Harry Styles.

    The property really is a dream home, spanning 5,148 square foot over four floors, with all the amenities Taylor could ever want within a prime location in Manhattan. According to Trulia, the townhouse has a master suite with walk-in wardrobes and dressing rooms, along with two more bedrooms, a guest suite, gym, theatre and private terrace.

    The Great Room is on the first floor, and features a large skylight to allow plenty of natural light to stream in. The room is currently furnished with colourful velvet sofas and armchairs, and leads on to the kitchen, which is fitted with limestone heated floors and Italian cabinets.

    Photo: Trulia

    The kitchen is stylishly decorated, with dark wooden Italian cabinets, limestone heated floors and state-of-the-art appliances. The spacious room will be ideal for Taylor when entertaining her friends, with a long wooden dining table and further stools lining the kitchen.

    Photo: Trulia

    On the lower level of Taylor's home there are several amenities on offer, including a gym, spa steam and bar, not to mention this theatre room. With several reclining leather armchairs, it would be an ideal spot for Taylor to enjoy private date nights with boyfriend Joe Alwyn, or catch up on the latest movie releases at home.

    Photo: Trulia

    There are two bedrooms on the second floor of the townhouse, which each have custom wardrobes and en suite bathrooms with Italian limestone baths, a jet tub and waterfall showers. There is antique French wide plank oak flooring and a neutral colour scheme in this room, which means Taylor will easily be able to put her own stamp on it.

    Photo: Trulia

    While this room is currently decorated as a children's bedroom, it could easily be transformed into a spacious and contemporary guest room, with views out into the popular Tribeca neighbourhood. This bedroom also has an en-suite bathroom, oak flooring and custom wardrobes and is on the second floor of the four floor property.

    Photo: Trulia

    The bathrooms are modern and pristinely decorated, with an Italian limestone bath and jet tub, along with a waterfall shower cubicle. This suite has a double sink and wall mounted mirrors, with plenty of space for Taylor or her guests to store all of their toiletries and beauty essentials.

    Photo: Trulia

    Taylor's townhouse also has something many properties in Manhattan don't – an outdoor terrace. This planted terrace features Japanese paper glass walls and a natural gas grill, along with an al fresco dining table and seating, ideal for when Taylor's girl squad come to visit. Can we get an invite?

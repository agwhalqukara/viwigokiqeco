Keith Tkachuk Wife: Who Is Chantal Oster?

Chantal Oster, is the wife of American ice hockey player, Keith Tkachuk.

Chantal had met Keith in Winnipeg as far back as 1995 and they eventually tied the knots in 1997.

The couple have stayed married since then and together they have three children — Matthew, Brady, and Taryn.

Chantal does not have much of her personal information available on the media space.

Who Is Chantal Oster’s Husband Keith Tkachuk?

Keith was born on March 28, 1972, in Melrose, Massachusetts.

Cheryl looks gorgeous in new L'Oreal campaign

Cheryl Fernandez-Versini is looking absolutely gorgeous in her latest campaign for L’Oreal Paris UK.

The Geordie babe is starring in the latest beauty advertisement to advertise the company’s latest product; L’Oreal Paris Infallible Sculpt Collection.

The new make-up product from the brand is for contouring.

In the new promotional shots from the X Factor’s latest campaign, the ‘I Don’t Care’ hitmaker is looking stunning wearing a structured black gown in one shot and a summery blue gown in another.

The latest advert was shot before Christmas and follows the tutorial video that Cheryl starred in for the False Lash Sculpt Mascara.

https://youtu.be/uhCm6n1QcMI

Speaking about the latest make-up advert for L’Oreal, Fernandez-Versini says:  “Contouring can completely re-shape someone’s face. Over the years I’ve learnt lots of different techniques from make-up artists. In my new campaign for L’Oréal I’m excited to share some of my top tips and tricks for a subtle, natural, sculpted look.”  

The new Infallible Sculpt palette is available from Boots for only £6.99.

The new tutorial video starring Cheryl for the new product is expected to be released soon.
